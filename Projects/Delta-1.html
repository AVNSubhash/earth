About Independence Day

Independence Day is celebrated every year on August 15th (In India). We got independence from the British rule on August 15th, 1947. It marks the day we got independence. On Independence Day, the Prime Minister hoists the Indian Flag at Red Fort, New Delhi. On that day, a parade is held at Red Fort in which many people from different parts of the countries show the tradition of their state. Many Soldiers from Army, Navy and Airforce march at the parade and some soldiers show the nuclear weapons made in India.
